We live with our hens 5 storeys up in central London.
We are lucky to have access to outdoor space where we have created a green oasis in the city, and our unique situation played a big part in our decision to keep hens.
Lots of people in cities keep hens in a variety of ways; some in gardens or terraces that they have access to, some in shared community spaces as well as schools.
