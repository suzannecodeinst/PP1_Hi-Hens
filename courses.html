Learn with us

We live with our hens on the fifth floor of a block in central London.
We are lucky to have access to outdoor space where we have created a green oasis in the city. Our unique situation played a big part in our decision to keep hens.
Lots of people in cities keep hens in a variety of ways; some in gardens or terraces that they have access to, some in shared community spaces as well as schools.
Understanding the suitable qualities of your lifestyle and environment is key.

Hentroduction - 1 day

A one day workshop to introduce you to all things hen held in our home. You will see how we set up our space, the decisions we made, the things we tried and the ideas we ruled out. Course includes...

  • Space requirements and basic essentials.
  • Choosing your hens and where to get them.
  • Handling hens, basic anatomy and health.
  • What to feed them and what to avoid.
  • Pests and predators.
  • Neighbourly considerations.
  • We provide a light lunch and refreshments.
  • A take home pack with notes and FAQs.

Dr Hen - 1/2 day

A half day course for a deeper dive into hen health.
This is held in our home with our hens on hand to help you learn. This course is veterinary led and includes...

  • What makes a healthy hen.
  • The nitty gritty; worms and lice.
  • Health checks and what to look out for.
  • First Aid.
  • When to call a vet.
  • The pecking order and hen psychology.
  • Bird Flu and legal requirements.
  • We provide a light lunch and refreshments.
  • A take home pack with notes and FAQs.

Hen House 1-2-1

We can help you assess your space and talk through your plans to house hens. We can visit you at your home (London area within the M25 only) or can provide an online review after you provide us certain details. We will be clear if we think your situation is unsuitable and offer suggestions for alternatives with local groups and communities. If all looks good we will compile a report with our recommendations for what you need and how to get going with organising your space and number of hens.
